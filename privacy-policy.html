AniKot Privacy Policy

AniKot respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your data.

Data We Collect

As an open-source app, AniKot does not collect any personal information from its users. This includes information such as:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Device ID
  • Device type
  • Operating System

How We Use Your Data

Since we don't collect any personal information, we don't use any data for specific purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

Data Security

While we don't collect personal information, we still prioritize the security of your device and data. We recommend using reputable security practices on your device to protect your overall privacy.

Children's Privacy

AniKot is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Please review it periodically.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, please contact us through the app's support channels or the open-source repository.
