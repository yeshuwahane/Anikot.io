Privacy Policy - AniKot App

Effective Date: [19/12/2024]

1. Introduction

Welcome to AniKot, an open-source mobile application showcasing anime content, built with Kotlin Multiplatform. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share data when you use AniKot.

2. Information We Collect

AniKot, as an open-source app, does not collect any personal information directly from you. This means we don't collect data like:

  • Username (if applicable)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (since the app is free)

However, we may collect some non-personal information automatically, which helps us improve the app experience:

  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device, such as the device type, operating system version, and unique identifier. This helps us understand how different devices run the app and identify potential compatibility issues.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage data about your interaction with the app. This includes features you access, frequency of use, and overall app performance. This data helps us improve user experience and prioritize features based on user behavior.

3. How We Use Your Information

The non-personal information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing Accurate Information: Location data (if enabled with your permission) can be used to display localized anime content relevant to your region.
  • Optimizing App Performance: Device and usage data helps us analyze app performance, identify bugs, and ensure smooth operation across various devices.
  • Improving User Experience: We use anonymous usage data to understand how users interact with the app and identify areas for improvement. This helps us prioritize features and functionalities that enhance the overall user experience.
